// Operation: Book The Job

How The Mission Runs

Step 1

AI CSR talks to customer

The AI CSR engages a customer who wants to request an emergency service — calmly, clearly, and in your brand's voice.

Step 2

AI books a job straight into ServiceTitan

The AI books the job and assigns the on-call technician on the dispatch board in ServiceTitan (or any other CRM you use).

Step 3

AI triggers the on-call technician & manager escalation flow

A built-in escalation flow fires a phone call and text message chain to alert the on-call technician and manager — no lead left behind.

Step 4

AI logs the call and keeps the office informed

The AI files a full after-action report — an email with the complete transcript and summary — for the service team to review during office hours.
